Why you are in good hands

With over 600,000 students worldwide and more than 10 years of experience, GEL is the leading provider of online learning for English as a Foreign Language. GEL supports many of the top language schools on five continents.

Why our materials are better

Our materials are based on the analysis of literally thousands of past questions, and are professionally written by experts. Then we test them thoroughly. Because GEL works with many very large IELTS preparation and test centres, we can easily test to discover what works best.

Our writing team

You can be sure our content is the very best. Our professional team of writers consists of published course book authors and well-known test writers as well as IELTS experts. The team comes up with interesting but appropriate topics, and develops them in specific ways to truly emulate real IELTS questions.

About GEL technology

GEL IELTS preparation uses proprietary technology to deliver Smart modes, so that you study the right things and progress faster. We don’t just give you a list of practice materials to do. GEL Smart modes give you Guided mode, which uses analytics to give you the best possible practice and Mock mode to simulate test conditions. With GEL IELTS prep, you don’t just do work – you do the right work.

Answer Types

GEL IELTS uses Answer Type analysis, a proprietary GEL system which understands what each question is testing – for example, whether you can find details in a text. Answer Type analysis understands why you make mistakes, and knows how to teach you to avoid them. It uses a proprietary algorithm to give you the best mix of exercises to help you prepare.

Quality control

GEL is a well-established publishing company working full-time in the field of English as a Foreign Language – we are not just a couple of guys writing in our spare time. Materials management commissions professional EFL writers for specific needs. Their writing is then checked by managing editors and verified against our proprietary Answer Type Analysis before final copy editing and publication.
